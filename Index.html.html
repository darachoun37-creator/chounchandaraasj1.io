Get Frest Food
in a Easy Way

Getting fresh food is simple if we follow some easy steps. First, go to the local market or supermarket where fruits, vegetables, meat, and fish are sold daily. Second, check the color, smell, and texture of the food to make sure it is fresh. Third, buy only the amount you need so it will not spoil. Finally, keep the food in a clean place, like the refrigerator, to stay fresh longer
